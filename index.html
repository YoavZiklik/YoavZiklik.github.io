Internship Experience

Last summer, I completed an internship at Unique Minds. During my internship, I worked on building components of an application used in a printing pipeline. This involved developing features related to generating printable layouts, handling data, and supporting workflow logic.

Through this experience, I gained exposure to real-world software development practices and learned how different parts of a system must work together reliably. I also saw firsthand the power of AI and how it can positively impact people, including at the Washington State Disabilities Fair.

The internship helped me better understand how programming looks in the real world and what jobs in software development are like.
